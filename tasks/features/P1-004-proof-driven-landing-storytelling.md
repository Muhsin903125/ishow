# P1-004 - Proof-Driven Landing Storytelling

## Summary

Replace the current generic comparison/progress imagery with a more human, respectful proof system that hooks visitors emotionally.

## Problem / Background

The current results section is visually active, but it does not feel specific to iShow. Generic fitness stock images do not build trust. The replacement should move toward a more honest editorial style and show what changed in clients' lives, not only how their bodies looked.

This includes the user's requested direction around `wabi-sabi` and public confidence, but it should avoid shame-based framing or demeaning depictions of heavier bodies.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [x] the stock-image results gallery is replaced with real proof modules or approved placeholders designed for that purpose
- [x] proof content includes narrative outcomes, not only appearance outcomes
- [x] the art direction supports a `wabi-sabi` feel: imperfect, lived-in, human, and less glossy
- [x] at least one proof module focuses on real-world confidence or public presence, not only gym aesthetics
- [x] body-transformation content is respectful, consent-based, and non-humiliating
- [ ] the proof system can be extended through CMS content, structured data, or approved assets

## Affected Files

- `src/components/LandingClient.tsx`
- `src/pages/admin/cms.tsx`
- `public/landing/**`

## Notes

- Strong replacements for old comparison grids include:
- weekly check-in snapshots
- coach message excerpts
- milestone cards
- confidence-in-public story frames
- outcome badges such as stamina, consistency, clothes fit, strength, mobility, and energy
